{{ svg('addressbook') }} {{ _('New Address Book')}}

{{ _('Thunderbird 102 gives your contacts a serious upgrade! Moving from Outlook, SeaMonkey, or another Thunderbird installation is easier than ever. No add-ons required!') }}
